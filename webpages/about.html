About Us

Paw Patrol Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and abused animals in Zambales, Subic, Pamatawan.
We are a small group of dedicated volunteers who work tirelessly to provide a safe haven for animals in need.
We believe that every animal deserves a loving home, and we are committed to finding forever homes for our residents. We work with a network of adopters and foster homes to ensure that our animals find the perfect match.
